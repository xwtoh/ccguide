Anthology Portfolio is the platform which will document the outcomes of your co-curricular experiences, helping you to consolidate your experiences across the co-curricular space in your undergraduate years, and cumulating in a co-curricular transcript that will be meaningful for you and your future employer

On Anthology Portfolio, your community experiences are organised into Placements, which contain the Pre, Mid & Post Activity requirements as shown.


Each activity that you partake in will then be in its own Placement. If you have multiple CSPs, Internships or CCAs throughout your undergraduate years, you will end up with multiple placements.

  • On the start date of your CSP, you will be prompted to complete your Pre-Activity LO Selection, which is a Survey

  • Go into Anthology Portfolio, click on Upcoming (on the top bar below the Dashboard header)

  • There, you can view pending Surveys or Assesments. Click on Take Survey/Assessment that corresponds to your CSP.

  • Note: Look out for labels such as DUE, OVERDUE & DUE SOON

  • Alternatively, go into Anthology Portfolio, click on Menu (on the top right of the screen)

  • Click on My Placements, and click on the CSP you're active in, and scroll down till you find the corresponding Pre-Activity LO Selection Survey.
